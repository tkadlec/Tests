Mobilism 2015

27th of March, Amsterdam

Tickets on sale now

21 February 2012 · PPK

On Wednesday 9th of May, the day before the conference, we have organised two mobile web workshops:

  • Remy Sharp will give his Mobile Web Apps workshop, where you'll get a day of practical hands-on exercises for dealing with mobile web apps, mobile browsers, and tools.
  • Jason Grigsby and Lyza Gardner will give a more responsive design-focused workshop, where they'll teach you how to work with media queries, server-side device detection, content-first strategies, and mobile-optimized markup.

Both workshops are an affordable €350, and the maximum number of attendees for both is 20. This ensures personal contact with the teachers and other attendees. This price includes lunch.

Take a look at the workshop descriptions and buy a ticket if you like them.
